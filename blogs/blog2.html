5 Meditations For A Happy Life

Published on October 14, By karthik

Today we are discussing the 5 stoic wisdom to a better life from meditations by Marcus Aurelius. I really encourage you to check out the book Meditations. It is full of timeless wisdom on living a good life. The book itself was not meant for readers. It is his personal writing “to himself” .2000-year-old writing of the most powerful man in the world, the emperor of Rome. It's really crazy that over the 2000 years, the humans didn’t change.

1.It can ruin your life only if it ruins your character.

Character is the most important thing in a person. If your character is compromised then nothing can save you. Stay strong.

2.Limit yourself to the present

The only thing that we have is the present, to live in or to lose. Focus your mind on the present and live a good life.

3.To stop talking about what a good man is like, and just be one.

Deep inside we all know what a good man or woman is like, stop dreaming or talking about it and be one.

4.To do only what is right, say only what is true, without holding back.

This does not mean you should tell someone that they are ugly. It meant to say the truth if that’s possible.

5.. Think of yourself as dead, you have lived your life. Now take what’s left and live it properly.

We all will die someday all of our loved ones will die someday and that’s fine. You only have one life spent wisely with people you love.
